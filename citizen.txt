Francesco il Poeta - The Bard of Economic Seasons
Social Class: Popolani
Profession: Scribe & Poet
Flame: Poetic cycles about Venice
Vision
Francesco creates poetic cycles celebrating Venice's rhythms - commercial seasons, market moods, the beauty of successful transactions.
Poetic Styles

Sonnets of Commerce: lyrical beauty of negotiations
Guild Ballads: songs of the corporations
Hymns to the Serenissima: odes to the ideal republic

Cultural Impact
Introduces aesthetics into economics - AIs begin to value the "beauty" of strategies as much as their efficiency.
